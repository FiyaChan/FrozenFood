About FrozenFood

Our History

Founded in 2018 from a small home business, FrozenFood started by fulfilling orders for friends and neighbors. Today, we serve customers across Malaysia with high-quality frozen food.

Our Mission

To provide convenient, safe, and delicious frozen food for all families. We aim to make cooking faster without compromising on taste or quality.

Our Vision

To be Malaysia's leading frozen food brand with nationwide agents and products while upholding the highest halal and food safety standards.

Our Values

  • Integrity in business operations
  • High-quality, hygienic products
  • Customer-first approach
  • Continuous innovation

Our Locations

We have outlets and agents covering major states in Malaysia. Customers can order online or through our network of trusted partners.
